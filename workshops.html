Workshop Empowerment

An empowerment workshop is all about the feeling of having control over your own situation.Empowerment is the driving force behind self-management. It allows you to determine your own life;
that is inner leadership. Empowerment is dynamic and can change through personal development, (life) experience or training, for example.

Content workshop



For many young people these are questions such as; who am I, what am I capable of, where is my passion and how can I use my core qualities in my daily life? This workshop has been specially developed to give young people more insightand to give them a grip on these issues. Examining one's own dreams and wishes are central to this. Both in the field of work (future) and private life, everything is discussed extensively. On the basis of non-formal education, the application of creative and playful working methods, the young people gradually become aware of their own talents and qualities during this workshop. The participant thus gains more insight into himself or herself and it is clear to him or her what steps to follow.

Our main points are:

  • Anti-bullying projects
  • Teambuilding
  • Motivation
  • Inspiration
  • Dreams and wishes
  • Reflect on your own behavior
  • Providing tools and practicing team building and collaborating with others
  • Encouraging individual responsibility and active citizenship
  • Stimulating (self) compassion and possible self-help.

Empowerment has six aspects

  • Competence: "self-confidence, knowing what you can do"
  • Self-determination: "know what you want"
  • Impact: “you participate”
  • Meaning: "important to you"
  • Positive identity: "you are OK"
  • Group orientation: “you are not alone”

We focus on team building, inspire to get more understanding and support together.
This workshop is tailor-made. Depending on the purpose and application of the workshop, the workshop can vary.
