Empowerment workshop


The feeling of gaining influence over your own situation is the focus of an empowerment workshop.
Self-management is fueled by empowerment. Inner leadership helps you to take charge of your own life.
Empowerment is dynamic and can shift as a result of personal growth.
ENOA wants to help develop you into the person you truly want to be.
